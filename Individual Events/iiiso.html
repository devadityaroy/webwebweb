IIISO

IEM Innovacion 2022 invites intellectuals from all around the globe to participate in the IEM Innovacion International Science Olympiad (IIISO), to test and showcase their analytical, logical, and critical thinking skills and talents in various fields of science including physics, chemistry, and biology on an international level. Compete only with the best of the best and prove your mettle on this unique platform provided by IEM Innovacion 2022.
